ABOUT ME

As a child, I always had a fascination with computers and the technology that powers them. Growing up in Delhi, I was exposed to the fast-paced lifestyle and the ever-evolving technology landscape of the city. I spent countless hours tinkering with computers, exploring the depths of the internet, and experimenting with programming languages. As I progressed through my academic journey, my love for computer science only grew stronger. I was determined to pursue a career in this field and eventually secured admission to BITS Pilani, which has been a dream come true for me.
